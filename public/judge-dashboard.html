Instruction

  1. Select a Contest: From the list below, click on the contest you wish to judge.
  2. Choose a Segment: After selecting a contest, you will see a list of segments (e.g., Sportswear, Talent). Click on the segment you are ready to score.
  3. Evaluate Candidates: On the judging sheet, you will find a card for each candidate.
  4. Enter Scores: For each candidate, enter your score for each criterion in the designated input field.
  5. Submit All Scores: Once you have scored all candidates for the segment, click the "Submit All Scores" button at the bottom of the page.
